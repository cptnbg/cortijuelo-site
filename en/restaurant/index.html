The Restaurant

Antigua Venta El Cortijuelo, open since 1948, sits 2.5 kilometers from Villanueva del Trabuco and near the springs of Los Cien Caños. The inn was acquired by Francisco Romero Mejías in 1948 and has stayed open ever since, serving over the years as a roadside inn, guesthouse, shop and restaurant. From 1948 to 1958 it ran as a lodging house for muleteers who stopped here along their routes. In 1958 Francisco passed it on to his son-in-law, Eugenio Romero, who took advantage of the steady passing trade and the location by adding a small shop and shoemaker's, making it even more popular. And so it has remained through the years to this day, now run by his children.
